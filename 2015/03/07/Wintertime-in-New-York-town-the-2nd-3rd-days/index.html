Wintertime in New York town - the 2nd & 3rd days

Lincoln Center

More arts! Like I said, you can take the subway and just stop at key places you want to see - like Times Sq (I gues, if you like that kinda thing!) or beautiful, wonderous, magical center for all the performing arts any persons’ heart could desire, The Lincoln Center

Soup at Epicerie Boulud - Daniel Boulud’s Upper East Side market/cafe/fromagerie. Terrible coffee - by Australian standards, but great for a quick, warm bite and very yummy green lentil soup with jambon-filled half sandwich for Rob.

Broadway!!

The center of the universe for shows (well one of the centers of the universe!). Half Tix fulfilled the dreams of an eleven year old Olivia Jones listening to a copy of the original Broadway cast burned from someone at school and imagining Caroline O’Connor on stage in black lace as Velma Kelly. Chicago at the beautiful old Ambassador Theater between Broadway and 8th was amaze - the big band on the stage and every singer a triple threat. Loved it!

Olivia Jones
